The beefy Dark Star 0degF synthetic bag from The North Face offers superior warmth during arctic and high-altitude climbing expeditions

  • Pertex(R) 20-denier nylon ripstop shell is lightweight and durable, and offers silky softness that enhances the insulation's loft
