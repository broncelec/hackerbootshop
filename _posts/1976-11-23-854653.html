  • What makes this Triclimate unique is that it's designed in a longer length and the shell fabric has a heathered look, giving it a more urban appeal
  • HyVent(R) shell is waterproof and breathable and insulated with Heatseeker(TM) insulation, offering a great warmth-to-weight ratio and is highly durable and compressible

  • 300-weight sweater fleece has zippered handwarmer pockets
