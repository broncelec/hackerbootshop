  • Female-friendly features include narrow shoulder straps, small hipbelt and short torso length
  • Mesh Verti-cool(TM) back panel circulates air between back and pack
  • Dual-density padded shoulder straps and 3-point adjustable hipbelt distribute weight for comfortable, stable carrying
  • Aluminum V-stays transfer load weight to the waist for comfortable carrying; polyethylene framesheet provides support and protection

  • Stretch-woven side pockets, plus a hydration pocket (reservoir sold separately) let you carry plenty of water
