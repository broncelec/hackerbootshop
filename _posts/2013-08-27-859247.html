Medium-flexing, intermediate-level boots that personalize the fit with a custom-moldable shell, Salomon X Pro 100 ski boots offer high precision for steep terrain and fast turns

  • Custom-moldable shells adapt to your feet for a superior fit that eliminates pressure points and improves performance; requires specialized equipment best done by boot fitters
  • Two-part "Twinframe" construction is composed of a rigid lower shell for power transmission and precision and a more supple upper shell for comfort
  • Rigid lower shell provides power transmission where it's needed in the heels, along the sides of the feet and in the binding contact areas
  • More supple upper shell offers appropriate rigidity above the instep, in the progressively flexing cuffs and in the supportive and flexing backbone of the shell
  • Oversize 25mm pivot between bottom shell and upper cuff results in greater transmission and power distributed throughout the sole of the boot compared to typical 8mm pivots
  • Pre-shaped liners grip ankles and heels without too much pressure, and custom-moldable foam conforms under feet, over insteps and along shins for superior foothold
  • The Salomon X Pro 100 ski boots have a medium flex index of 100, providing the responsive power transfer appropriate for intermediate skiers
