Trail-worthy and affordable, the REI Lookout 40 pack comfortably handles a broad range of outdoor adventures, whether you're enjoying a cool forest hike or a done-in-a-day summit scramble

  • Updated shoulder straps and hipbelt provide a soft and ergonomic fit while stiff load-carrying sections preserve comfort under heavy loads
  • FreeFlow back panel features multiple ventilation channels that allow cool air to flow across your back
  • Spring steel frame transfers weight to the hipbelt and stabilizes the load from unwanted side-to-side movement; perimeter design allows the back panel to conform to your back
  • Panel-loading main compartment makes it easy to organize your gear; bottom zipper adds easy access
  • Hydration pocket can be accessed from the pack's exterior, allowing you quick access to refill your reservoir (sold separately) without digging into your pack
  • Small zippered pocket with soft lining provides an ideal spot for storing delicate items such as sunglasses or electronics
  • Innovative water-bottle pocket design allows a bottle to be stored in an easy-to-reach position, then rotated and locked in place for secure storage
  • Trekking pole attachments feature adjustable lower loops that capture the pole tips, while an elastic quick-clip securely fastens your poles to the pack
  • Simple, pulley-style side compression straps compress more of the bag than traditional single straps and with less extra weight and material than dual straps
  • Quick-release compression straps on the bottom provide an easy attachment point for sit pads or other bulky items
  • Lightweight lash points allow additional gear to be strapped to the front of the pack
  • The REI Lookout 40 pack features a thermoplastic elastomer bottom panel that provides waterproof durability to withstand years of heavy use
