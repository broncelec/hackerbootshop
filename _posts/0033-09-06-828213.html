The beefy Dark Star -20degF synthetic bag from The North Face offers superior warmth in frigid, subzero conditions and high-altitude climbing expeditions

  • Pertex(R) 20-denier nylon ripstop shell is lightweight and durable, and offers silky softness that enhances the insulation's loft
  • Pertex shell features a Durable Water Repellent finish that causes water to bead up and roll off
  • Climashield(R) a continuous filament insulation, provides warmth, durability and water resistance in a lightweight package
  • Overlapping shingle construction distributes insulation evenly and keeps it from moving around so there are no cold spots
  • Vaulted footbox allows natural foot movement and eliminates excess lateral space, increasing thermal efficiency to keep your feet warm and comfortable
  • The North Face Dark Star includes a compression stuff sack that doubles as a summit daypack
