Our premium cold weather bag, the REI Magma sleeping bag features goose down insulation and a waterproof, breathable shell for protection from damp and chilly nights

  • Tested by an independent third-party lab using European Norm protocol, the EN temperature ratings allow direct comparisons across brands with other EN-rated bags
  • Waterproof, breathable shell is an ultralight, coated ripstop nylon designed to dissipate moisture from the inside and repel moisture from the outside, keeping the down dry
  • 800-fill-power goose down is efficiently distributed for maximum warmth and compressibility; ultralight polyester mini-ripstop lining has a soft hand-feel
  • Exclusive REI Down Fill Calculator optimizes the amount of down per baffle for the most warmth with the least amount of down
  • 60% of insulation is put on top of bag where it contributes the most warmth and loft
  • In-the-round pattern wraps vertical baffles around perimeter of sleeping bag, from head to foot, creating a 3-dimensional bag that wraps you in warmth
  • Performance fit saves weight by reducing girth at shoulders, hips and lower legs while increasing thermal efficiency
  • Baffle spacing is optimized to keep down in place and prevent cold spots
  • Multichambered contour hood, insulated full-face muffler, and full-length zipper draft tube with ground-level seams all work to keep heat in and cold out
  • Anatomically contoured draft collar drapes lower in the front and higher in the back for a better fit; cinch adjustable to seal in warmth
  • Trapezoidal footbox mirrors sleeping position for room to move and thermal efficiency
  • External chest pocket keeps small essentials-headlamp, watch, glasses-right at hand and easy to find in the dark
  • Includes nylon stuff sack and large cotton storage bag
  • Differentiated drawcords (1 round, 1 flat) allow quick identification, even in the dark, so neck and hood adjustments are a cinch
