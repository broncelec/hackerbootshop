SLX M662 is a 9-speed, top-normal rear derailleur that features Shimano Shadow RD design for superior trail clearance and crisp shifting

  • Shadow RD design is intended for aggressive riding; a low profile and single-tension construction prevent the derailleur from hitting the chainstay in rough riding conditions
  • The result is smooth, silent performance and increased shifting durability
  • Straight cable design ensures crisp and accurate shifting
  • Strong guide spring prevents chainstay contact
  • 11-tooth pulleys run quieter and wear longer than typical derailleurs thanks to alternating tooth contact between inner and outer chain plates
  • Fluorine-coated 2-pin bushings reduce link-pin friction resulting in reduced shifting effort
