The REI Passage 1 is a classic, freestanding dome tent with an X-pole design

  • Passage 1 offers a simple setup and low weight for 1 backpacker
  • Asymmetrical shape and side entry vestibule maximize interior volume and livable versatility
  • Easy setup using equal-length aluminum poles, quick-attachment clips and color coding
  • Angled poles maximize usable interior volume
  • Seam-sealed polyester fly ensure a waterproof, durable and sag-free shelter
  • Seam-sealed insider floor provides the waterproofness of a bathtub floor and the taut pitch of a cut-in floor, combined with the abrasion resistance of nylon
  • No-Wick welded construction ensures that moisture won't wick through susceptible areas such as guyout points, pole wraps or zippers in wet conditions
  • Fully adjustable, high-level vents mitigate condensation, drawing cool air under the fly from the lower perimeter and exhausting warm, moist air
  • The REI Passage 1 tent offers multiple interior storage options provided by various combinations of mesh side pockets and hang loops located throughout
  • Packaged in compression storage/carry sack with 5 stakes and 2 guylines and tensioners
