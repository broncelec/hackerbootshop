The Look Pivot 14 XXL bindings are designed to enhance your performance, increase your safety and create an unparalleled connection with your skis

  • Why settle for less?
  • Remember last year when you hit a small bump, your ski randomly popped off, and you went down like a freight train?
  • To prevent such mishaps, Look has engineered key features into the binding to reduce premature release, the most innovative of which is a rotating heelpiece
  • Look calls it the Turntable heel, and it rotates with the boot directly under your tibia, creating consistent and reliable release
  • Pivot also features 40mm of elastic travel at the toe and 25mm at the heel which absorbs shock and allows a lower setting of release values
  • Look has reduced the space between where the heelpiece and toepiece are mounted on the ski, increasing responsiveness
  • 3 contact points between the bindings and the heels ensure effective power transfer
  • 72mm anti-friction device increases leverage and control, especially on wide skis
  • 117mm brake fits most all-mountain wide and some powder skis
