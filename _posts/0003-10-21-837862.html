With the sleek REI Side Country 19 snow pack, you can get away from the tracked-out runs and access the best parts of the mountain

  • Perfect for a day at the resort, the REI Side Country 19 offers enough room for safety equipment, snacks and an extra layer
  • Tarpaulin interior resists moisture and abrasion
  • Front zippered pocket is sized for a shovel blade, and features a sleeve for the handle
  • Back panel material sheds snow and dries quickly
  • Skis carry diagonally across the front of the pack, and snowboards and snowshoes carry vertically down the center; straps tuck away
  • Padded front panel and tough reinforcement patches prevent damage from skis and snowboards
  • Clip on top of pack provides a place to attach your helmet and keep it out of the way while climbing
  • Hydration pocket is designed to accept most hydration reservoirs, reservoir not included
  • Insulated sleeve on shoulder strap stores the hydration hose to help prevent freeze-up and is large enough to accommodate right-angle shut-off valves
  • Hipbelt snugs comfortably to help distribute weight to your hips, not your back
  • Hipbelt webbing system adjusts with a forward pull, creating a secure, ergonomic fit
  • Snow-specific buckles are designed to work well with gloves on and to prevent clogging with snow
