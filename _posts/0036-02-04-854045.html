The REI Trail 25 pack transitions smoothly from the wilderness to the city with durable construction, all-day load support and a modern design with easy organization for urban gadgets or outdoor gear

  • Soft, conforming shoulder straps are comfortable and incorporate stiff load-carrying zones for heavy loads
  • Removable hipbelt and sternum strap let you lighten up or stabilize your load as you need
  • Smooth ventilated mesh in critical contact areas provides ventilation and comfort
  • Internal HDPE framesheet adds structure and helps shield your back from the contents of the pack
  • Panel-loading main compartment holds your hiking or commuting essentials and includes a sleeve and tube port for a hydration reservoir (sold separately)
  • Organizer panel sports multiple pockets for pens, electronics and small essentials; front pocket and side mesh pockets offer on-the-go access to bottles, sunscreen and snacks
  • The REI Trail 25 pack has pulley-style compression straps to cinch down loads for jostle-free carrying; lightweight lash points let you strap gear to the front of the pack
  • Cordura polyester fabric offers durability for years of heavy use; TuffCoat bottom panels add waterproof durability and resistance to abrasion
