Built for tough mountaineering challenges and long backcountry tours, the powerful Fritschi Diamir Vipec 12 Randonee Bindings with Brakes balance ski performance, ease of use and reliable release

  • Similar to the release system found in downhill bindings, the lateral release in the toe units occurs in 2 phases to release when you need it to but not when you don't
  • Heel units are on gliding plates that ensure the bindings have consistent contact pressure on the boots, even when the skis are significantly bent
  • Getting in the bindings is quick and easy-simply move the tips of your boots forward over the step-in bracket in the toe units and press down to click in
  • Unlike other tech-style bindings, the toe pieces on the Vipec bindings will release even when you're in uphill touring mode, but only when you need them to
  • Quickly switch back-and-forth between ski and tour mode by flipping the rear levers up or down with a pole; there's no need to release from the bindings to switch modes
  • Nonrotating heel pieces create a very stable platform for excellent control and power transfer to the skis
  • Heel pieces include flip-up climbing bars that can be changed easily with a ski pole; raise your heels to 9deg or 13deg depending on the steepness of the hill
  • Bindings include ski brakes to prevent runaway skis; choose from 120mm- or 108mm-wide brakes depending on your ski width
  • Choose the size that's just wider than the underfoot width of your skis; for example, choose 108mm size for 105mm-waisted skis
  • Fritschi Diamir Vipec 12 Randonee Bindings with Brakes have adjustable DIN release settings from 5 - 12
  • Compatible with boots featuring tech system toe and heel attachments, such as those found on all Dynafit boots and select styles of Garmont and Scarpa boots
  • Compatible with the Fritschi Diamir Traxion Crampons, not included
