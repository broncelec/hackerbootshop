Built for freeriders, the Burton Barracuda snowboard features a special rockered tip for enhanced board float on deep snow days, whether dropping pillow lines or traversing across the mountain

  • S-Rocker(TM) applies a unique contour to the Barracuda; entry rocker extends from the nose to under your front foot, and then transitions to camber between the feet
  • Slightly tapered shape and directional flex combine with S-Rocker to create a floater that goes up, over and through anything
  • Compressing the camber causes the entry rocker to naturally lift the nose, improving float while maintaining speed through deep-driving pow turns and variable conditions
  • Nug Raduction optimizes every element of the board, from flex profile to sidecut, letting you downsize up to 10cm from your normal board length
  • Nug Raduction boards ride the same as a traditional length board, but offer a surprisingly stable and responsive ride; Burton recommends sizing Barracuda down 2 - 3cm
  • The Channel system offers unlimited, on-the-fly stance adjustability, enhances board feel and is compatible with EST(TM), Re:Flex(TM) and Disc(R) bindings
  • The Channel lets you fine-tune your stance width to the millimeter, and then dial in your toe-to-heel centering and rotate your angles in one fell swoop
  • Wood core is snappy, powerful and durable; dual-zone wood grains, positioned along the toe and heel edges perpendicular to the core, enhance edge hold and add strength
  • Fiberglass in the topsheet provides versatile flex and response so you can shred your big mountain descents
  • Lightweight Carbon I-Beam(TM) riding up the board's spine adds to board's longitudinal snap
  • Tapered thickness in tip and tail reduces swing weight and improves powder flotation
  • Edges extend out slightly beneath the bindings for tremendous edge hold in hard, icy conditions, but they remain playful and forgiving in softer conditions
