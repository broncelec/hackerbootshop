Speed up the fine search for buried victims with the Pieps iProbe ONE probe

  • It works like a standard avalanche probe with the added feature of being able to detect the signal of a buried transceiver
  • iProbe One has a built-in receiver in the tip that can detect any brand of avalanche transceiver; probe emits an audible alert when you're close to the victim
  • When the tip is within 2m the probe emits "piep, piep" and when it's within 50cm it emits a continuous "piiiiiiiep"
  • If the buried transceiver supports iProbe technology, the transceiver's signal can be temporarily suppressed by the iProbe to make searching for other victims easier
  • Probe tubes are made of high-quality carbon fiber and aluminum for good strength at a light weight
  • Centimeter markings help you determine burial depth
  • Grippy coating ensures you have a good hold on the probe, even when wearing gloves
  • 1 AA battery (included) powers the Pieps iProbe ONE probe for up to 150 hrs
  • Includes a storage sack
