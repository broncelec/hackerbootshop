  • Clove hitch the sling to 2 anchor points then use the buckle to adjust the length of the sling to equalize the anchor
  • Girth hitch the green loop to your belay loop on your harness to use sling as an adjustable personal anchor
  • Edelrid Adjustable Belay Station Sling can also be used as an adjustable etrier for aid climbing
