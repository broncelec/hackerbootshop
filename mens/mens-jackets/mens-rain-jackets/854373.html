Description:

The REI Havenpass jacket with eVent(TM) fabric is perfect for an epic climb to basecamp, alpine touring in a powder-filled bowl or pushing the pace on a snowshoe trek

  • eVent fabric has a unique membrane structure that allows sweat vapor to easily pass through the fabric; so you can go all out, yet stay drier and enjoy chill-free rest stops
  • eVent technology works so efficiently, no pit zippers are needed; all seams are sealed for complete waterproof protection; the shell is also windproof to 60+ mph
  • Embedded Recco(R) reflector enhances radio signals from search-and-rescue Recco detectors for quicker acquisition of position in the event of an avalanche
  • Helmet-compatible hood is adjustable to provide unhindered peripheral vision; drawcord system also snugs down to fit over your head alone
  • 2-way front zipper lets you adjust fit and venting; zipper is backed by a stormflap and topped by a chin guard
  • Adjustable hem drawcord and rip-and-stick cuff tabs let you fine-tune the fit
  • 2 zippered, mesh-lined front pockets are placed to clear hipbelt; 2 zippered, mesh-lined chest pockets can double as vents; 1 chest pocket has an inner cord port
  • The REI Havenpass jacket also has 2 inner stretch-mesh pockets that are large enough to comfortably hold climbing skins or other essentials
  • Note: To keep weight and bulkiness to a minimum, this backcountry jacket does not include a powder skirt
  • Care instructions: Machine wash warm with detergent (no fabric softener); rinse twice; a warm dryer will rejuvenate the Durable Water Repellent (DWR) finish
