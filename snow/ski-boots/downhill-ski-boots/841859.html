Description:

Featuring a very comfortable fit, the women's Nordica Transfire R1W ski boots drive the rockered and cambered shapes of modern skis with precision

  • The Nordica Transfire R1W uses a new shell design to enhance side-to-side control, offering precise lateral pressure to put modern skis on edge
  • Boots flex lightly at first, then as you put greater pressure on tongues during turn, the resistance increases and enhances precision
  • After flexing, boots return to upright position instantly, setting you up for the next turn
  • In addition to flexing progressively, the Transfire R1W boots feature a block in the spine of the boot that ensures the boots do not engage the skis too quickly
  • Getting your foot in the boot is hassle free thanks to a forgiving 3-piece shell design
  • Padding in the tongues and cuffs absorbs shock and damps vibrations, making your ride smooth and easy
  • As compared to men's liners, women-specific liners feature extra padding in the tongues, more insulation throughout and contoured top edges designed to fit women's calves
  • Liners snugly wrap the feet in cushioning foam and warm faux-fur lining; closed-cell foam against the shell enhances warmth
  • Liners provide a personalized fit around heel, ankle and center of foot when custom molded
  • Middle buckles clamp down on feet at a 45deg angle to keep heels securely seated and increase comfort in forefeet
  • Power strap acts as a fourth buckle, allowing you to set the firmness around the upper ankle to favor comfort or performance
  • Double canting adjusts the cuffs to fit the angles of your lower legs and offers a versatile range of adjustment
  • 100 flex index provides the responsive power transfer preferred by expert skiers
  • 102mm-wide forefoot offers a great mix of comfort and precision
