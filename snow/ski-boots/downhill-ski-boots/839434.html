Description:

The Rossignol Vita Sensor2 70 ski boots will take you from tentative turns to confident shredding

  • Shell of the Vita Sensor2 70 is designed to enhance balance through direct contact with your big toe, little toe and heel
  • Stiff polycarbonate inserts under big toe, little toe and heel work with the shell to transfer energy to skis
  • Notches in the plastic along the instep create an active hinge system that makes it easy to get your foot in the boot
  • Women-specific liners are designed with a lower cuff than men's boots to accommodate women's calf muscles; liners are scallop-shaped in back to ensure a comfortable fit
  • Rossignol Vita Sensor2 70 liners address the common fit issues of cramped toes, pressure on the insteps and pressure on forefeet
  • 1-piece toe box and tongue eliminates seams and overlaps in the tongue attachment area, reducing pressure points at top of the toes
  • Foam padding in the liners resists packing out; smooth contours and an anatomic fit eliminates pressure points in forefeet
  • Specially designed insteps provide excellent midfoot fit, resulting in heightened responsiveness, increased circulation and greater warmth
  • Soft padding securely holds ankles and heels in place; liners are outfitted with plush, velvety material
  • Wool toe boxes help retain heat in your toes
  • Custom-formable liners can be heated in a specialty ski shop to achieve a personalized fit
  • 4 buckles feature a diagonal pull design that sets the foot securely into the heel pocket and enhances shell wrap
  • Buckles are microadjustable for an accurate fit; top 2 buckle catches provide 22mm of adjustability
  • Power strap serves as a fifth buckle, allowing you to set the firmness around the upper ankle as you prefer for comfort or performance
  • With a 70 flex index, Rossignol Vita Sensor2 70 ski boots provide light resistance against a forward lean, appropriate for most progressing skiers
