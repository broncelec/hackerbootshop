Description:

Built with hike/ski levers, the intermediate-level Atomic Waymaker 80 W ski boots for women feature roomy 101mm footbeds and flexible shell zones for comfort while you explore the sidecountry

  • Hike/ski levers release cuffs from lower shells to increase range of motion when hiking; return levers to ski position to lock down cuffs during the descent
  • Single-component shell moves as a single piece to maximize power transfer and ensure high performance, and pliable plastic flex zones adapt instantly to the shape of foot
  • Located between the first buckle and the toe, flexible shell zones made of soft plastic improve balance, circulation, warmth and sensitivity

  • Custom-moldable liners mold to ankles and forefeet to increase heel hold
