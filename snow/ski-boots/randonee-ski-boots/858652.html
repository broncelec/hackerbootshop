Description:

Balancing best-in-class features at a great price, the women's Scarpa Freedom randonee boots pair alpine performance with light weight and 27deg of cuff range for excellent tourability

  • Weighing in at only 3,522g (7 lbs
  • 12 oz) per pair for a size mondo 25, the Freedom boots are remarkably light among the freeride boot category
  • Boots employ a patented Carbon Core Technology, which molds the polyurethane lower boot around a carbon fiber frame to stiffen the boot without adding cumbersome weight
  • Overlap closure with two upper cuff buckles and two lower cuff buckles, and a low-profile 101mm forefoot last contribute great power transfer
  • Intuition FR liners put resilient, lightweight and warm foam into a multidensity alpine-style liner that matches the power and tourability of the boot
  • Forward lean is 14deg, with plus or minus 4deg of adjustability
  • Vibram(R) injected rubber outsoles provide good grip when you're walking
  • Included interchangeable DIN soles are compatible with all alpine bindings and randonee bindings except Dynafit-style tech bindings
  • Optional Vibram Mountain Plus soles (sold separately) make the boots compatible with tech bindings
  • Scarpa Freedoom randonee boots include adjustable spoilers and oversize power straps
