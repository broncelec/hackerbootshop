Description:

Rip down the resort or head out on a backcountry tour with the powerful Scarpa TX Pro telemark boots

  • They're compatible with NTN telemark bindings and Dynafit-style randonee bindings for versatility
  • 100% Pebax(R) plastic construction provides high stiffness and power without a lot of weight; Pebax plastic maintains a consistent flex in all temperatures
  • NTN boots do away with the duckbill traditionally found on telemark boots, making walking and scrambling easy; boots are compatible with NTN telemark bindings, sold separately
  • Boots also feature tech fittings at the toes and heels to allow use with Dynafit-style bindings-that's right, you can ski free- or fixed-heel with the same boots! Custom-thermal-formable Intuition Speed Pro G liners use dual-density foam for great performance, comfort and support
  • Tongue-style liners (as opposed to a wrap-style liners) provide easy entry and comfortable flex through the shins when touring and making telemark turns
  • Pull tabs on the tongues and rear top edges of the liners ease entry
  • Liners can be custom molded to your feet by an REI ski shop professional for the optimal fit
  • 4 magnesium buckles are strong and light; asymmetric tongues transfer power precisely
  • Second buckle from the bottom on each boot is designed to secure your foot and heel in place; thicker cable than on previous versions improves durability
  • Wide rip-and-stick power straps wrap securely around the outside of the cuffs, distributing pressure and bringing the cuffs as close as possible your lower legs
  • Rear ski/walk mechanisms free the cuffs for walking comfort; flip the switches to ski mode and you can lock the cuffs at 14deg or 20deg of forward lean
  • Scarpa TX Pro telemark boots feature lugged Vibram soles for good grip while hiking on rock and snow
