Description:

The Burton Custom Flying V snowboard is the all-mountain board you've come to know and love

  • It blends rocker and camber, giving you the best of both worlds when riding both steeps and park features
  • Rocker between and outside the feet enhances playfulness and float in pow; subtle camber zones underneath your feet combine with edge-gripping tech to add pop, snap and power
  • Burton Channel system offers unlimited, on-the-fly stance adjustability and is compatible with traditional Burton 3D(R) bindings
  • The Channel lets you fine-tune your stance width to the millimeter, and then dial in your toe-to-heel centering and rotate your angles in one fell swoop
  • Wood core is snappy, strong and responsive; wood grain positioned along toe and heel edges is perpendicular to the core for superior edge hold and strength
  • Fiberglass energy strands radiate outward from the Channels to the board's edges, providing poppiness and responsiveness without stiffening the board
  • Lightweight Carbon aligned with the board's spine adds to board's longitudinal snap
  • Squeezbox tech transfers energy outward from under your feet, energizing the tip and tail for added snap when exiting a turn and ollieing
  • Additionally, Squeezbox creates a more stable board that can be powered up and manipulated with less effort
  • Edges extend out about half a millimeter underneath bindings for tremendous edge hold in hard, icy conditions, and are tuned to slice and dice the entire mountain
  • Pro-Tip(TM) edges are tapered at the tip and tail, reducing swing weight and improving powder flotation
  • Highly-porous sintered base is ultra durable and has specially formulated wax infused into the pores for long-lasting glide
