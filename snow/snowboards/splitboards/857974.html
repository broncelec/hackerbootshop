Description:

With a shape geared toward backcountry riding, the K2 Ultrasplit splitboard snowboard is built to ride what you crave: deep, light and fluffy pow

  • Tweekend baseline profile features a flat base between your feet and a rockered tip and tail for effortless float in the pow
  • BC shaping features a longer tip blend zone for more control; compact sidecut locks a turn at any speed; longer tail blend and shorter tail give you control in tight trees
  • Directional twin shape provides plenty of overall float and control, offering a balanced ride that's equally versatile ridden regular or switch
  • Bamboo is blended into the core for increased strength and added durability; carbon webs near binding area maximize ollie pop and control
  • Fiberglass laminated in directional layers adds torsional stiffness, improving edge hold and increasing stability during off-axis landings
  • ICG 20 support integrates carbon into the fiberglass, reducing overall weight and creating a supersmooth release of power
  • Sintered Base is an ultrathin, super-conductive base; lightweight with lower material usage, it's the highest quality sintered base offered by K2
  • K2 Ultrasplit features the all-new K2 Split Track, which lets you use the all-new K2 Kwicker system (sold separately), based on the Clicker system from years ago
  • Also works with standard bindings or splitboard bindings; requires the Voile Universal Splitboard Binding Interface to mount bindings, sold separately
  • Skins sold separately
