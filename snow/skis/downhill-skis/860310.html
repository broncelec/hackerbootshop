Description:

The advanced-level Volkl RTM 77 skis with bindings tear up the groomers with mellow rocker and offer enough waist width to float on soft snow when conditions are right

  • When tipped over and put on edge, the smooth, gradual rocker from tip to tail creates uninterrupted edge contact during the turn, ripping graceful arcs into groomed terrain
  • Full rocker also increases maneuverability in soft snow, so you can play on the backside of the mountain or hunt down overlooked stashes of pow
  • Raised reinforcement channels along the top edges of the skis, a distinct feature on all Volkl carvers, make the skis feel extra stable at high speeds
  • You'll also notice a lively, snappy feel when powering through turns thanks to the steel laminate built into the dual wood core
  • Bindings attach to skis via integrated rails, eliminating screws and promoting superb edge hold and natural flex
  • Integrated bindings feature DIN 3 - 11 release settings, a range suited to intermediate or advanced skiers at medium to high speeds
