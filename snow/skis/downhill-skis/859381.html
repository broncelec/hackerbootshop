Description:

With stiff laminates in the core to smooth the ride, the advanced-level Rossignol Temptation 88 women's skis use tip and tail rocker to readily take an edge on groomers and shred through soft snow

  • Poplar wood core creates light and lively skis that transmit the feel of the snow and put you in touch with the terrain, and stiff basalt laminates reduce vibrations
  • Varying shapes of laminates reduce the weight of the tips, making them easier to control
  • Layered laminates in tips also smooth out turn initiation, improve edge contact during turns and increase side-to-side response
  • Subtle rocker in tips and tails offers easy steering in any snow conditions, and traditional camber underfoot provides tenacious edge grip
  • Extended sidecut continues beyond the rockered tips and tails, providing full engagement of ski edges during turns at high speeds and easy maneuverability at low speeds
  • Vertical sidewalls deliver excellent power transfer for superb grip on hardpack snow
