Description:

The Deuter Freerider Pro 30 pack brings together legendary Deuter durability with snow-specific options galore for pursuing powdery pleasure in the backcountry with skis, board or snowshoes

  • Multiple backcountry setups let you carry a snowboard or snowshoes vertically, or skis vertically, horizontally or A-frame style
  • Removable, pivoting hipbelt follows your every move with fluid ease, helping you keep your balance and preserve energy on uneven terrain
  • Adjusters on the shoulder straps fine-tune the fit; loosen them for comfort during approaches and quickly tighten them down for precise load control
  • Two vertical, air-channeling foam strips on the back panel ensure maximum circulation and stability; removable foam mat creates additional support and can be used as a seat
  • Back-panel access lets you get to the main compartment's contents when lashed-on gear blocks access from the front, and helps you avoid letting snow in
  • Hydration-compatible design accommodates a reservoir and tube (sold separately); removable loops and helmet holder let you secure tools and a helmet to the exterior
  • The Deuter Freerider Pro 30 pack has stretch side pockets, a front shovel pocket with probe sleeve, 3 internal zippered valuables pockets and a fleece-lined goggles pocket
