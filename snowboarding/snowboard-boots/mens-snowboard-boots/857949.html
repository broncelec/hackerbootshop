If you're a hard-charging, all-mountain rider serious about performance, Vans Infuse snowboard boots offer top-of-the-line features, an amazing fit and classic Vans styling

  • Vans Hybrid Boa lacing offers the best of both worlds: it combines traditional laces with a Boa closure that focuses primarily on the critical instep area of the boots
  • This configuration allows you to lace your boots tighter than with traditional laces only, and it also allows on-the-fly instep adjustments for boot flex
  • Custom Boa dials celebrate the partnership between Vans and Boa, and feature a diamond texture and sidestripe detailing

  • Full-grain leather uppers feature a waterproof treatment for all-day protection from the elements
