With a soft flex made for the park and extra-soft highbacks that move with you, Burton Infidel snowboard bindings are finely-tuned bindings built for throwing down tricks

  • Single-component, lightweight yet tough, polycarbonate baseplates create moderate flex and consistent response for a versatile ride
  • Single-component, Gumbo highbacks are ultra responsive; minimized design saves precious weight, and they rotate to follow natural leg contours, optimizing comfort and power
