Description:

The Burton Sherlock snowboard solves the mysteries of the deep, untracked pow

  • Take this board out on a freshie-filled day and enjoy the ride
  • Sherlock is built for riders who don't hesitate to pick their own lines; it's a shock-absorbent, all natural jib and jump machine that floats like a dream
  • Flying V(TM) is a premium blend of camber and rocker, and offers the best of both worlds in terms of performance
  • Rocker overall, and between and outside the feet, enhances catch-free playfulness and float through pow
  • Subtle camber zones underneath feet focus edge control for crisp snap, added pop and power through the turns
  • Directional twin shape offers the freestyle feel of a twin board but is slightly tweaked to provide improved float and control through variable terrain and conditions
  • Surface area at the tip and tail contact points is lengthened to improve edge-hold, and allows the board to plane easier in powder
  • The Channel system offers unlimited, on-the-fly stance adjustability, enhances board feel and is compatible with EST(TM), Re:Flex(TM) and Disc(R) bindings
  • The Channel lets you fine-tune your stance width to the millimeter, and then dial in your toe-to-heel centering and rotate your angles in 1 fell swoop
  • Wood core is light, snappy and responsive; engineered wood grain positioned along the toe and heel edges, perpendicular to the core, improves edge hold and strength
  • Lightweight Carbon I-Beam(TM) riding up the board's spine adds to board's longitudinal snap
  • Fiberglass in the topsheet provides versatile flex and response so you can shred everything from big mountain descents to laps in the park
  • Tapered tip and tail thickness reduces swing weight and improves powder flotation
  • Meaty 10:45 sidewalls optimize 2 different angles to absorb impact for aggressive riders
