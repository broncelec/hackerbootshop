Description:

This Marmot CWM EQ bag is designed for extreme cold, from polar regions to high-altitude mountaineering in the Himalayas, with the highest quality 800-fill-power goose down and a waterproof shell

  • Marmot MemBrain(R) ripstop nylon laminate shell is waterproof, breathable and downproof
  • MemBrain technology reacts as the temperature inside your sleeping bag rises, increasing its breathability to keep you dry and comfortable
  • Lofty, premium 800-fill-power goose down offers superb warmth for minimal weight; It's meticulously cleaned and processed for hypoallergenic qualities
  • 9-baffle chest section provides maximum goose down control for warmth and durability
  • 6-Baffle Hood envelops your head in warmth; Face Muff allows hood to be cinched snug without digging into your face
  • No more midnight drawcord fumbling-smooth, flat upper cord and hard, round lower cord make hood adjustments easy
  • Hood closure system stays closed without the use of rip-and-stick and also keeps the drawstring and zipper away from your face
  • Spacious 11-baffle footbox allows down to loft fully; footbox is reinforced to handle extra abuse of boot liners and water bottle
  • Down-filled draft collar increases warmth; hidden pocket in down-filled draft tube keep small items such as lip balm or your watch warm and accessible
  • Zipper guards prevent zipper snags; locking, full-length zippers keep bag from opening in the night
  • Comes with nylon stuff sack and a storage bag
