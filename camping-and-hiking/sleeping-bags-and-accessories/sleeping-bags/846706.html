The Big Agnes Lost Ranger sleeping bag features water-repellent down insulation so you can rest easy even at the first sound of raindrops on your tent

  • DownTek(TM) 650-fill-power down repels water by employing nanotechnology and a proprietary process that applies a micro-thin polymer to the down
  • DownTek down repels water and maintains loft and insulation value, even when exposed to moisture
  • Proprietary application process creates a water repellent, antimicrobial and antibacterial down insulation that actually floats on water
  • Insotect Flow(TM) Construction features internal Flow Gates(TM), which are vertical chambers with internal mesh gates that keep insulation from shifting up and down
  • Underside insulation has been eliminated and replaced with a sleeve for a 20 in
  • wide Big Agnes rectangular pad or any 20 in
  • wide pad, sold separately
  • Continuous draft wedge insulates the connection between the bag and pad to prevent cold air from entering
