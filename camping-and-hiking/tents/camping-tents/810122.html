Description:

A home-away-from-home, the REI Hobitat 4 tent is a spacious and easy-to-use campground tent

  • Hobitat 4 features 1 generously sized end door
  • If your activities center around the campsite, the Hobitat 4 provides a comfortable daytime private refuge and a nighttime shelter from the wind, the rain and the critters
  • Rectangular floor plan with end-to-end symmetry makes it easy to arrange sleeping bags and gear and provides organizational efficiency and comfort
  • Combination of pole clips, pole sleeves and color coding facilitate a quick and easy setup
  • Advanced frame design maximizes headroom, interior volume and strength
  • Huge door with 4 zipper sliders provides multiple entry configurations that can be personalized for both children and adults; door tucks away into an adjacent pocket
  • Weatherproof door with awning provides easy access, ventilation and good weather protection
  • Seam-sealed polyester fly provides a waterproof, durable and sag-free cover; seam-sealed, cut-in floor provides a taut pitch and waterproof protection
  • Roll-up side panels mated to interior bug-proof mesh panels enhance and allow you to control ventilation
  • Multiple interior storage options are provided by various combinations of mesh side pockets and hang loops located throughout the tent
  • Includes carry/storage sack, 8 stakes and 4 guylines with tighteners
  • Connect Tech zipper attachment lets you add more living space or storage with the Hobitat Garage or Hobitat Vestibule, both sold separately
  • Please note: 2011 Hobitat tent will not work with prior years' Hobitat Vestibule
