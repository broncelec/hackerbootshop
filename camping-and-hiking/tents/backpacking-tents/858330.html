Description:

The Mountain Hardwear SuperMega UL tent is designed for backcountry adventurers who travel fast and light

  • It's the lightest 2-person backpacking tent in the Mountain Hardwear lineup! At just over 2 lbs
  • the SuperMega UL 2 offers no-compromise weather protection and a generously sized vestibule
  • Conveniently-located wide end door makes access easy for both campers
  • Dry-entry vestibule design prevents water from dripping inside tent when vestibule is open, improving livability
  • Unique cinch cord on vestibule door allows quick tightening adjustments without the need to remove and restake the guyout; simply pull the cord to keep the fabric taut
  • Freestanding lightweight design provides a no-hassle setup
  • All fabrics used are designed to be as lightweight as possible while still delivering solid protection from the elements
  • DAC Featherlite(R) NSL poles utilize unique inserts to create strong, functional poles that save weight and reduce breakage
  • Lightweight power clips at all pole intersections increase structural strength
  • Welded zipper flap construction is lighter and drier than a sewn flap
  • Perimeter seams form waterproof tub-style floor that is up off the ground and under the rainfly for extra weather protection
  • Mountain Hardwear Watertight Construction: Thoughtful design, taping and welding technology combine to make this tent watertight off the shelf
  • No seam sealing is required
  • Mesh pockets help keep tent organized
  • Reflective guyout loops, starter points and zipper pulls enhance nighttime setup and visibility
  • As a convenient option, poles and rainfly can be set up with the footprint (sold separately) to create a lightweight shelter
