Description:

The REI Crestrail 48 Pack nicely balances comfort, organization and durability in a backpack designed for weekend trips

  • REI ActivMotion(R) suspension features a pivoting hipbelt that helps maintain load balance over uneven terrain
  • Tubular aluminum frame cuts weight, transfers load to hips and lets the pack conform to your back shape
  • REI's Freeflow Comfort back has generously padded sections, plus sculpted ventilation channels and a central breezeway to help keep you cool
  • Adjustable torso length lets you fine-tune the fit
  • Over-the-top compression strap pulls loads closer to your back; side compression straps also help snug down the load
  • Hydration pocket is accessible from outside, for quick access to a reservoir (sold separately); fits up to 3-liter reservoirs and you can run the sip tube over either shoulder
  • Top lid has multiple pockets; 2 have soft tricot linings for scratch-free storage of phones or sunglasses
  • Side zip offers access to deep-down gear
  • Stretch mesh front pocket is handy for bulky items
  • Up to a 32-oz bottle can be twist-locked inside each side pocket; a drawcord adjusts the pocket opening
  • Smooth, moisture-wicking fabric covers body contact areas to help you stay drier and more comfortable when your exertion level picks up
  • Tip grips are paired with speed hooks to securely hold trekking poles; pack also has multiple lash loops
  • Bottom compression straps can be used to attach a sleeping pad
  • The REI Crestrail 48 Pack also includes hipbelt pockets that are handy for stashing trail essentials
