Description:

The CamelBak Fourteener 20 Hydration Pack is a compact and technical pack for done-in-a-day alpine adventures and everyday outings

  • 100 fl oz Antidote(TM) reservoir features a welded center baffle and low-profile shape for stability
  • Quick Link(TM) system lets you effortlessly add accessories such as a Fresh(TM) filter, insulated tube, tube director or flow meter (accessories sold separately)
  • Hydration compartment loads easily, holding the reservoir flat against your back to leave more room for layers and gear
  • Wide mouth makes it easy to fill and clean the reservoir; quick-snap cap tightens in just a quarter turn
  • Folding arms lock into position to hold the reservoir open when drying; arms tuck out of the way around the port when not in use
  • With its separate zippered opening, the reservoir can be easily refilled without removing it from the pack
  • Harness with padded shoulder straps, sternum strap and hipbelt provide maximum comfort and even load distribution
  • NV(TM) back panel features upper pods that articulate for maximum comfort and superior ventilation, while lower pod provides load transfer
  • Main compartment holds lunch, gear and all your 10 essentials for a 1-day peak ascent
  • Large, zippered front pocket keeps valuables close at hand; small zippered top pocket with fleece lining stores sunglasses or MP3 player
  • Dual side-compression straps help stabilize the load; tool loop and lash point let you attach extra gear to the exterior of the pack
  • The CamelBak Fourteener 20 Hydration Pack is made from rugged, polyurethane-coated nylon for long-lasting wear
