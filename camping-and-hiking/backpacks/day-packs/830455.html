Description:

The lightweight Black Diamond EXL pack is a prime choice for fast trail pursuits and ridge scrambles with all-day comfort, clean design and the perfect volume for your essential gear

  • SwingArm(TM) shoulder straps move in concert with a hiker's stride or a climber's reach by sliding through the bottom of the pack with a low-friction cable and housing
  • Shoulder straps are linked to the other by the cable which helps maintain an even, balanced load across the shoulders
  • Webbing hipbelt helps stabilize the pack; padded back panel features perforated, mesh covered foam and an air channel for enhanced breathability
  • Zippered panel access to the main compartment provides access to layers, water and other essentials
  • Hydration-compatible design accommodates a reservoir and drink tube (sold separately) for on-the-go hydration
  • Side stretch pockets hold gels or snacks; front compression cord secures rain gear or other items
  • The Black Diamond EXL pack features a retractable trekking pole/ice axe loop and bike light slot that lets you lash gear to the exterior of the pack
