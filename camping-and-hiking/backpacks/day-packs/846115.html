The formfitting Salomon Advanced Skin S-Lab 5 Set hydration pack is built for intense trail running

  • Includes a 50 fl oz (1
  • 5 liter) Hydrapak(R) hydration reservoir so you can quickly satisfy your thirst on long runs; reservoir is phthalate free, PVC free and BPA free
  • Drink tube routes under your right arm and the bite valve is held in place for easy access while you're running; on/off switch on the bite valve prevents leaks
  • SensiFit construction uses stretchy fabrics to allow the pack to conform to your body for a good fit and great stability while you're on the go
  • 2 pockets on the shoulder straps hold water bottles (not included) within easy reach; removable zippered pocket on the left shoulder strap keeps energy gels and bars handy

  • Quick-disconnect valve lets you remove the drink tube when you want to clean the reservoir
