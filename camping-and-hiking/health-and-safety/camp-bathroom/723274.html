Description:

Compact and portable unit delivers hot water anytime for cooking, washing and other camp tasks, giving you more time to enjoy the outdoors

  • Using standard 16
  • 4-ounce propane cylinders and a rechargeable water pump, unit can produce up to 40 gallons of hot water per cylinder and per charge
  • Includes sturdy polyethylene 5-gallon water container for water storage; pump hose attaches neatly to container for use
  • Easy and quick to use: connect hose to water container, adjust to desired temperature and within 5 seconds hot water is ready for use
  • Sturdy plastic casing with carry handle stands up to rugged camp life; water spigot emerges from handle for easy direction of hot water
  • Storage panel on side holds included AC adapter and DC cigarette-lighter adapter for recharging of pump battery
  • Replaceable lead-acid 6V battery supplies reliable power to water pump; 40-minute run-time on pump and approximately 8 hours to recharge battery
  • Front indicator lights for Over Temp, Battery Low and Recharging keep you informed of appliance's status
  • Electronic ignition via piezo igniter for simple, matchless heating; heater generates up to 30,000 BTU and water temperatures up to 160deg F
  • Low maintenance unit requires occasional cleaning with simple household solution
  • Unit is to be operated outdoors only in well-ventilated areas to prevent suffocation from carbon monoxide
  • Can be set up to use bulk propane tanks and continuous water supplies using appropriate adapters (not included)
  • Hot Water on Demand(TM) is designed to be used on a raised, level surface such as a picnic or camp table; do not operate directly on the ground
  • Use only potable, purified drinking water as unit will not filter or clean water in any way
  • Due to flammable item shipping restrictions, we cannot sell fuel canisters online; they are available at REI retail stores
