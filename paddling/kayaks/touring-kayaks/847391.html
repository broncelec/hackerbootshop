Description:

With the stability and agility of a recreational kayak and the space and efficiency of a light touring kayak, the Wilderness Systems Tsunami 125 kayak is sized for day and overnight trips

  • Slightly larger than its 12 ft
  • cousin, the Wilderness Systems Tsunami 125 kayak is a great choice for large paddlers thanks to its spacious cockpit
  • Hull is crafted in high-density polyethylene for amazing durability and easy care
  • Solid initial and secondary stability enhances confidence when learning, and speed and handling delight more experienced paddlers
  • Cockpit is easy to get into no matter who is paddling
  • Phase 3 Air Pro Tour seat offers excellent comfort and intuitive adjustments
  • Mesh stretched over contoured foam forms a perforated pad; large holes enhance airflow, and small holes enhance support for sit bones and lower back
  • Scalloped, contoured seat pan cradles and cushions legs, and backrest conforms to size of paddler and flexes a bit with every paddle stroke
  • Icons shows what each adjustment strap does, and all control points are forward of the paddler and centrally located to reduce strain
  • Leg lifter uses handle to shore up space below your legs; back strap lowers seat back, and the angle of back rest is adjustable as well
  • Small gear bag under seat features a gear clip and enough capacity for a water bottle or extra food
  • Rear bungee cords lock seat back forward during transport
  • Slidelock footrail system is easily adjustable, comfortable and rugged; footbraces adjust via extension rod that places positioning at your fingertips
  • The Tsunami 125 kayak is equipped with 2 generous hatches plus plenty of deck rigging, letting you easily stow a weekend's worth of food and gear
  • Bulkheads in bow and stern along with rubber hatch covers keep gear secure and dry and assist with flotation
  • Domed hatch covers offer a rugged watertight seal and an easy-to-remove tab and contours
