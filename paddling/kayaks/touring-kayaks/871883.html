Description:

Sized for small-framed paddlers, the Current Designs Solstice GTS Fiberglass Kayak with Rudder has excellent acceleration, is highly efficient and can hold plenty of gear for trips big or small

  • Incredibly light, strong and stiff, the vacuum-bagged fiberglass layup eliminates excess resin, which improves strength and reduces weight to 52 lbs
  • Shallow-V hull enhances tracking and offers friendly stability and a lively feel, low decks minimize wind resistance, and fish-form shape enhances flotation in the surf zone
  • Keyhole-shaped cockpit provides a responsive fit, yet it's still easy to get into
  • Built-in thighbraces on the underside of the rim maximize control when edging and rolling
  • Seat back with foam backpad creates cushy comfort during long hours on the water
  • Cockpit-deployable rudder enhances tracking in rough weather; use the footbraces to direct the rudder and steer the kayak
  • Hatches provide ample storage for an extended trip and feature easy-to-use compression hatch covers
  • Compression hatch covers sit flush with the deck and feature a rubber gasket that creates a watertight seal when tightened with cam straps
  • Current Designs Solstice GTS Fiberglass Kayak with Rudder comes with bow, stern and center bungee deck rigging, toggled carry handles and internal rudder cable routing
