Description:

The innovative Boreas Bootlegger Modular Pack System features 1 Super-Tramp suspension and 3 packs, so you can customize your pack choice for every adventure, wet or dry

  • Need a dry bag for water or snow outings? Click the Super-Tramp suspension into the waterproof, fully submersible Scrimshaw so you can wear it; or simply use it as a bag
  • The Scrimshaw roll-top "do anything" dry bag/pack has a 30-liter capacity and is designed to fully contain the Hopper pack to keep it dry in bad weather
  • The Hopper 28-liter daypack is ideal for daily commutes, day hikes or travel; it has hydration sleeve and dual ports, and 2 front stretch pockets
  • Hopper also has a waterproof top pocket for your cellphone and wallet; 4 compression straps and a hidden daisy-chain for attaching gear; and a reflective screen print
  • The Torpedo is an ultralight, stripped-down 9-oz hydration sleeve that holds a 100-oz(3L) bladder (not included) and has dual hose ports and zippered top pocket
  • Torpedo offers a 13-liter capacity, just big enough for hydration and a few small essentials for summit hikes, trail runs or cycling
  • Super-Tramp suspension includes aluminum frame with stainless steel stays, mesh back panel, perforated EVA foam shoulder straps with air mesh backing; adjustable hipbelt
  • Trampoline-style suspension has straps to adjust the tension, giving you either more air space between the pack and your back, or less to keep it close when off-trail
  • Easily attach the suspension to any of the 3 packs in the Boreas Bootlegger Modular Pack System and you're good to go
