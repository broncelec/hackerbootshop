The compact and lightweight Exped Mountain Lite 20 Pack feels right at home from foothills to mountain peaks or on a long climb

  • Versatile front-compression design reduces volume and secures gear
  • Removable, slim hipbelt carries weight without obstructing a climbing harness; shoulder strap webbing is secured to the base for load control, even without the hipbelt

  • Variable-position hipbelt buckle can be centered or, depending on personal preference, worn left or right over the padded belt to prevent pressure on your abdomen
