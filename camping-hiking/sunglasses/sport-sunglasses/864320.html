Description:

The rimless Smith PivLock V2 interchangeable sunglasses feature lightweight, interchangeable lenses that let you adapt to changing light conditions during your active sports

  • Ideal for very bright light, Blackout (gray-tinted) lenses allow you to see objects without color distortion
  • Frames include 2 additional sets of lenses: photochromic Ignitor (red-tinted) for medium light/overcast conditions and clear for dim light
  • Photochromic Ignitor lenses automatically adjust tint according to light conditions, and eliminate glare and enhance contrast while maintaining accuracy of color
  • With the PivLock interchangeable system, simply rotate temple pieces up 45deg and remove from lens; to remove nosepiece, grasp lens and pull piece away
  • Scratch- and impact-resistant polycarbonate lenses use Smith Tapered Lens Technology(TM); the lens is progressively tapered so that light travels through without distortion
  • Permanent lens coating repels moisture, grease and grime to keep your vision clear
  • Frames are made from a bio-plastic derived from non-GMO castor seeds and lightweight nylon for a resilient, consistent fit
  • Hydrophilic temple and nose pads become tackier when wet for reliable grip during exercise and hot weather; slide-on temple tips
  • Adjustable nose bridge lets you customize the fit for narrow, medium or wide bridges
  • Smith PivLock V2 interchangeable sunglasses are medium-sized with wrap-around coverage, and include a protective case
