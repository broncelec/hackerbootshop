Compact, lightweight and designed for use with narrow cord, the Nite Ize CamJam Cord Tightener cinches, anchors, rigs and secures lighter loads securely, quickly and easily

  • CamJam threads narrower cord (from 1
  • 8 - 3mm in diameter) for, you guessed it, smaller jobs
  • With a stainless-steel gated carabiner on 1 end, and a rotating, ridged cam mechanism on the other, the CamJam can be anchored to any hook, D-ring or loop
