Description:

The ultralight, freestanding Big Agnes Seedhouse SL3 tent is for 3 backpackers (or 2 and a dog) who like to count ounces and still have a little elbow room to read at night

  • Seedhouse SL3 is simple, efficient and classic; it's built for adventures like cross-country bike tours or those weekend peaks you've been looking to bag
  • Hub/pole system is super lightweight and tent body attaches quickly and easily to the pole frame with plastic clips; fabric loops along the ridge pole increase weight savings
  • Innovative pole design provides exceptional headroom without excess weight
  • 1 large door allows easy entry/exit for 3 occupants
  • Walls are constructed of woven nylon mesh, providing excellent ventilation and 360deg views
  • Rainfly and seamless floor are made of high-tenacity ripstop nylon and are both polyurethane- and silicone-coated for waterproofness and durability
  • Light yet strong, DAC Featherlite(R) NSL pole system with pressed-in connectors and lightweight hubs are anodized without the use of harmful acids
  • Includes 4 interior mesh pockets and loops for a gear loft (holds a Big Agnes Large Trapezoid Loft or Entertainment Center Loft, not included)
  • All seams on tent body and fly are sealed with waterproof, solvent-free polyurethane seam tape; vestibule stormflap protects zipper from the elements
  • Big Agnes Seedhouse SL3 includes stuff sacks, 11 superlight aluminum J-stakes, and reflective guylines and webbing on tent corners
  • Save weight and create a minimalist shelter by using just the rainfly, footprint (sold separately), poles and stakes
