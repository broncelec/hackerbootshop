Description:

The women's Marmot Sunset 30 synthetic-fill sleeping bag features innovative construction to keep you warm from top to bottom, and it has nearly the same weight and packability as a down bag! Designed to fit a woman's body, bag offers increased room at hips, decreased room at shoulders and extra insulation in critical heat-loss areas

  • Thermal R synthetic insulation's excellent loft keeps you warm with minimal weight; innovative wave construction, with overlapping shingles of insulation, prevents cold spots
  • Body-mapping of insulation directs warmth to key areas and maximizes thermal efficiency
  • With a European Norm (EN) comfort rating of 32degF, the Sunset 30 is a versatile bag for mild-weather 3-season adventures
  • Non-bulky Thermal R maximizes warmth while reducing the adverse effects of perspiration, condensation and humidity inside the sleeping bag
  • Anatomic 3D hood envelops your head in warmth; Face Muff allows hood to be cinched snug without digging into face
  • Trapezoidal footbox allows insulation to loft fully around your feet for optimum warmth and comfort
  • Full-length zipper with a snagless draft tube prevents fabric from snagging in the zipper coils
  • No more midnight drawcord fumbling-smooth, flat upper cord and hard, round lower cord make hood and collar adjustments easy
  • Marmot Sunset 30 comes with a lightweight stuff sack
  • European Norm (EN) is an independent third-party temperature rating, allowing accurate comparisons across brands with other EN-rated bags
