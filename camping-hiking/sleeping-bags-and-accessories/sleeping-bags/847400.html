Description:

The Nemo Rhythm 25 sleeping bag brings up the notion that maybe mummies didn't sleep all that comfortably

  • Nemo finds a middle ground for the living and breaks the mold of sleeping bag shapes
  • The new Nemo Spoon Shape is wide in the shoulders, tapers to hips and then gently flares back out to allow room for the natural bend and shift of knees
  • PrimaLoft(R) Synergy synthetic microfiber insulation works a lot like down: it minimizes bulkiness, packs down well and stays warm even when wet
  • Underside of sleeping bag features 10% less insulation than the topside to cut down on weight; insulation underneath is often flattened, thus less doesn't hinder performance
  • Waterproof/breathable coating on backside of fabric combined with a Durable Water Repellent on the exterior protects from tent wall condensation
  • A nifty little feature in the hood is the tuckable Blanket Fold(TM), an insulated flap of fabric that can be wrapped around neck to block drafts and add a layer of warmth
  • NEMO Rhythm 25 sleeping bag comes with a compression stuff sack and storage bag
