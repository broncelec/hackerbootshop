The REI Backpacker Multiday First-Aid Kit provides essential medical supplies for up to 4 people on 5-day trips into the backcountry

  • Reflective trim makes the kit easier to locate quickly inside a pack
  • Comes with a wilderness first-aid manual for reference on the trail, along with an accident record and pencil

  • strips, 0
