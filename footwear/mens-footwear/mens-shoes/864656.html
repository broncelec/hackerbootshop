Description:

The Brooks Adrenaline GTS 14 Road-Running Shoes keep feet happy through the miles, thanks to adaptive cushioning, responsive support and a nice heel-to-toe transition

  • Synthetic mesh uppers enhance breathability and quick-drying performance; synthetic overlays offer support
  • New lace design evens out the lace pressure to create a snug fit, while 3D Fit Print adds a lightweight, flexible, no-stitch fit
  • Nylon and spandex linings absorb and disperse moisture for enhanced comfort; Internal Support Saddle (ISS) helps wrap and hold feet in place
  • Midsoles feature BioMoGo polymer alloy foam, which provides cushioning, energy return and reduced foot fatigue; nontoxic additive helps midsoles break down in landfills
  • DNA cushioning uses high viscosity liquid to react instantly to your every step, providing a custom amount of cushioning for your running style
  • Progressive Diagonal Roll Bar triple-density medial posts work to control excessive pronation and balance transitions from midfoot to toe-off
  • Thermoplastic urethane arch shanks control torsional flex at midfoot for control and support
  • Forefoot Stable-Pod configuration balances midsole and outsole components to set feet in an efficient, balanced position to maximize stability and responsiveness
  • Outsoles on the Brooks Adrenaline GTS 14 Road-Running Shoes are constructed of a mix of rubber compounds to maximize longevity and grip
  • Outsoles also feature a special crash pad design that smooths out the landing zone, maximizing efficiency and feel throughout the heel strike
