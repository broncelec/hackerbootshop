Description:

The Saucony Ride 6 road-running shoes serve up plenty of comfort to neutral runners thanks to the highly breathable uppers and cushioned midsoles

  • Breathable nylon mesh uppers feature synthetic leather overlays for light support
  • Nylon linings and plush collars breathe well, wick moisture and dry quickly to keep your feet cool and comfortable
  • Breathable insoles are constructed from open-cell foam to provide plenty of cushioning underfoot; antimicrobial treatment deters odors
  • Responsive, dual-density EVA midsoles maintain rebound over the miles; strobel board construction increases cushioning and comfort
  • Thermoplastic urethane shanks control torsional flex at midfoot for light arch support
  • Special impact zones cushion heel and forefoot impact while setting foot for a smooth transition
  • 8mm heel-to-toe drop encourages a short, low-impact stride
  • Outsoles on the Saucony Ride 6 shoes use blown rubber under forefeet for cushioning and flex; carbon rubber at heels delivers traction and durability
