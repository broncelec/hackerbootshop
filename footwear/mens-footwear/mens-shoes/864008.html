The ASICS GEL-Lyte33(TM) 3 Road-Running Shoes get serious about weight reduction, dropping almost 2 oz per shoe over the previous version for a lightweight, natural running experience

  • Updated with nearly seamless uppers and Solyte(R) midsoles to maximize performance while minimizing weight
  • The use of seamless materials reduces the potential for irritation and friction caused by traditional stitches and seams
  • ASICS GEL-Lyte33 3 Road-Running Shoes are designed for underpronators and neutral runners
