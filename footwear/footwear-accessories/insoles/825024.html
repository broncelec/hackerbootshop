SOLE Chris Davenport Signature CD Thin custom footbeds have a low-profile, lightly insulated design that's optimized to fit in tight-fitting ski and snowboard boots for wintertime support and comfort

  • Heat-moldable design ensures personal fit and ample support to reduce stress and foot fatigue
  • Wicking, perforated polyester topsheets manage moisture and balance friction control with comfort; contains 38% recycled content
  • 400g layer of Thinsulate(TM) insulation offers enhanced warmth underfoot, keeping toes toasty
  • Deep heel cup and customized arch support help maintain stability and proper knee-to-ankle alignment to reduce fatigue and pain
  • Custom fit helps eliminate pressure points by supporting the arch and distributing weight evenly to increase blood flow and reduce numbness
  • Can be worn with or without heat-molding to achieve a custom shape and the proper amount of support
  • Heat-moldable EVA foam base supplies lightweight cushioning and a custom shape; contains 20% recycled content
  • It's very easy to mold the footbeds: simply warm footbeds in a 200degF oven for about 2 minutes, then remove and place inside footwear

  • To finish the process, step into the footwear and stand straight with feet shoulder width apart for 2 min
