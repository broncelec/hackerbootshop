Description:

The heat-moldable SOLE Thin Sport custom footbeds are designed to be used in low-volume footwear like ski boots and cycling shoes, offering support and stability underfoot

  • Heat-moldable 3-layer design ensures personal fit and ample support to reduce stress and foot fatigue
  • Deep heel cup and customized arch support help maintain stability and proper knee-to-ankle alignment to reduce fatigue and pain
  • Also helps to support stabilizer muscles, which means more energy is dedicated to the muscles generating power
  • Custom fit helps eliminate pressure points by supporting the arch and distributing weight evenly to increase blood flow and reduce numbness
  • Can be worn with or without heat-molding to achieve the proper amount of support and a custom shape
  • Wicking, perforated polyester topsheets manage moisture and balance friction control with comfort
  • EVA heat-moldable foam base supplies lightweight cushioning and a custom shape
  • Easy molding: warm footbeds in 200degF oven for about 2 minutes, then remove and place inside footwear
  • Opti-Therm Molding indicator makes custom fitting a snap-indicator turns black when insoles are ready to be removed from oven
  • To finish the process, step into the footwear and stand straight with feet shoulder width apart for 2 minutes
