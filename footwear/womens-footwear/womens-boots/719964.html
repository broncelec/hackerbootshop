The La Sportiva Trango S Evo GTX mountaineering boots with Gore-Tex(R) waterproof protection feature all synthetic construction and a unique ankle-hinge system for ample support

  • Water-repellent Cordura(R) nylon uppers with Flex Tec 2 rubber-coated, Aramide-reinforced polyester panels are super durable and lightweight
  • Reinforced with water-repellent Lorica(R) synthetic leather for maximum durability and strength
  • Waterproof, seam-sealed Gore-Tex(R) inserts keep feet dry while allowing excess moisture vapor to escape to prevent overheating
  • Wicking Dri-lex(R) nylon linings absorb excess moisture and disperse it for fast drying and enhanced comfort
  • Fully-gusseted and padded tongues keep out debris and water while relieving lacing pressure
