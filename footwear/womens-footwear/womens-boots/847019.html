Description:

The Lowa Mauria GTX hiking boots are designed on a women-specific last and offer cream-of-the-crop comfort on your treks into the wilderness

  • Nubuck leather uppers are durable and flexible, offering long-lasting protection and comfort; rubber toe and heel caps protect uppers from abrasion
  • GORE-TEX(R) waterproof liners let feet breathe while protecting them from the elements, keeping them dry and comfortable
  • Moisture-wicking polyester linings offer excellent climate control within boots by moving sweat away from feet, helping to reduce the chances of blisters developing
  • Perforations inside cuff linings let heat and moisture out so your feet can enjoy a cool, dry and blister-free environment
  • C4 system uses an asymmetric padded tongue, soft edging, recessed ankle cut and curved insert for tailored comfort across instep
  • Tongue stud X-Lacing system establishes even pressure distribution over the insteps
  • Flex Fit construction facilitates natural ankle flex without compromising stability, resulting in a more comfortable stride
  • Polyurethane midsoles offer outstanding cushioning for all-day wear; midsoles allow for normal pronation but reduce excess cushioning to enhance stability
  • Lowa Mauria GTX hiking boots feature nylon shanks for enhanced torsional rigidity to protect and support feet
  • Vibram(R) Apptrail rubber outsoles feature aggressive lugs, self-cleaning tread and a pronounced edge at front of heel for excellent downhill traction
