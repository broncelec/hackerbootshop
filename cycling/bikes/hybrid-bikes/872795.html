Description:

With a rear rack, fenders and disc brakes for commutes or long rides, the Currie E3 Path+ Bike uses a powerful, 500W electric motor to zip up hills, cruise flats and reduce the stresses of riding

  • Powered by a rack-mounted, removable lithium-ion battery pack that you can charge anywhere
  • LCD showing speed, distance and time is mounted conveniently on the handlebar, and remote button lets you operate the bike without moving your hands from the handlebar
