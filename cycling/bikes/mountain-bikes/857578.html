Description:

A tough mountain bike for winding singletrack, the Novara Bonita women's bike features a nimble aluminum frame, powerful disc brakes and a shock-absorbent fork to smooth out rough trails

  • Lightweight 6061 aluminum frame features an S-bend downtube to enhance strength and stiffness at critical frame junctures
  • Suntour XCM V3 suspension fork gives you 80mm of travel to smooth out rough trails
  • Shimano Alivio crankset is paired with a wide-ranging cassette to power the 27 gear combinations, making easy work of conquering hills and cruising descents
  • Shimano Acera trigger shifters guide the Acera front and Alivio rear derailleurs for responsive, precise shifts
  • Tektro hydraulic disc brakes provide reliable stopping power and controlled deceleration
  • Mineral oil system in the hydraulic disc brakes performs consistently in all conditions and is a great alternative to DOT chemical fluid systems
  • WTB Nano Comp tires feature a fast-rolling center tread with more-aggressive traction at the edges for all-around performance
  • The Novara Bonita women's bike has an anatomically designed WTB Speed She Sport saddle for comfort and support
  • Just get on and ride, no fancy shoes required(TM)aluminum platform pedals accommodate a variety of shoes
