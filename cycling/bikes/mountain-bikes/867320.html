Fast-rolling 29er wheels, 20 speeds and a rear Fox shock with multiple modes for climbs and descents make the Diamondback Mason FS 29er full-suspension mountain bike a premier ride for any trail

  • The heart of the Mason FS 29er is the Knuckle Box platform, which lowers the center of gravity of the suspension to optimize efficiency and effortlessly absorb small bumps
  • Fox Float CTD Air rear shock can be easily tuned between the 3 CTD settings (Climb, Trail, Descend) for efficiency on any gradient with great protection from bottoming out
