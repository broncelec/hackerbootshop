Throwing a powerful 1,700 lumens of light in a compact, lightweight design, the CygoLite Centauri 1700 OSP front bike light offers smart customization to meet your nighttime riding needs

  • With 2 superbright Cree LEDs, up to 1,700 lumens of light is at your disposal to illuminate the road, path or trail ahead; light is optimized to illuminate both wide and far
  • OSP (On-Site Programmable) technology means you can set brightness levels and customize light flash speeds to suit your needs; all with the push of the button while riding
  • 4 total brightness and 4 special settings: boost, high, medium and low along with SteadyPulse(TM), day flash, slow flash and walking
  • SteadyPulse illumination mode combines steady brightness with an alerting flash, letting you see what's ahead of you while also letting others know you're present
  • Water-resistant aluminum casing helps ensure lasting performance
  • Illuminated display indicates which light setting is in use and a battery indicator displays level of remaining power; buttons are on top of light for quick and easy use
