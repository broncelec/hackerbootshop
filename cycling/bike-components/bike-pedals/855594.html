Description:

The Time Xpresso 4 bike pedals are light, easy to get in and out of and offer a high level of adjustability to offer good efficiency and comfort on your road rides

  • Instead of metal springs, flexible carbon fiber blades tension the retention mechanisms, ensuring a secure pedal connection while reducing weight
  • Getting into the pedals is a breeze, thanks to the low-tension engagement of the retention mechanism: step in with little pressure, pedal engages cleat, and then pedal away! Excellent foot-to-pedal stability and solid power transfer are achieved through the oversize pedal platform and low stack height (distance from shoe sole to pedal axle)
  • Light and sturdy composite pedal bodies spin smoothly around steel axles; replaceable stainless-steel wear plates enhance pedal durability
  • Foot/pedal interface can be tuned and optimized, offering adjustment to Q-factor (side-to-side), +/-5deg of angular float and 2
  • 5mm of lateral float; 15deg release angle
  • Time Xpresso 4 bike pedals come with cleats and mounting hardware; compatible with most road cycling shoes with 3-hole mounts
