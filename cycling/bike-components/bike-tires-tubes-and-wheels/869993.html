A 900g carbon wheel built for speed, the rear Zipp 303 Firecrest Carbon Clincher Disc Wheel offers 11-speed compatibility and a versatile clincher design that works with disc brakes

  • Ultralight carbon rim uses Firecrest(R) shaping to optimize the aerodynamic profile of both the front and back halves of the wheel
  • This rim shape also offers incredible crosswind stability, performing closer to a box-section rim despite the 45mm deep profile; dimpled surface dramatically reduces drag
  • Wider rim profile increases side-to-side stiffness for responsive sprinting and cornering; also offers great control, comfort, and strength
  • Zipp 188 hub uses Swiss-made cartridge bearings with extremely smooth-rolling grade 10 steel balls
  • Shimano/SRAM freehub body is compatible with 11-speed cassettes; works with 10-speed cassettes with optional shim (shim not included)
  • 24 spokes are laced radially on the drive-side and crossed on the non-drive-side onto the 188 hub
