Description:

Powerful Scarpa T1 women's telemark boots combine stiff shells, burly buckles and high-performance Intuition liners to give you maximum control for steering wide skis down steep lines

  • Boots feature Pebax Rnew(R) plastic that is made from castor plant oil rather than petroleum as most plastics are
  • Pebax Rnew performs exceptionally well across a wide range of temperatures, ensuring consistent flex, strength and stability
  • Torsionally-stiff plastic shells combine with 4 buckles and a power strap on each boot to deliver the control needed to drive big skis at high speeds
  • Custom-thermal-formable Intuition Speed Pro G women's liners use dual-density foam for great performance, comfort and support
  • Women-specific liners scoop down in the back to accommodate women's lower calf muscles; liners are premolded on a women's last for a great out-of-the-box fit
  • Liners feature flex zones on the tongues for good ankle articulation while making telemark turns
  • Pull tabs on the tongues and rear top edges of the liners ease entry
  • Liners can be custom molded to your feet by an REI ski shop professional for the optimal fit
  • Second buckle from the bottom on each boot has been improved over previous models with thicker cable to lock your feet in place and withstand the stress of backcountry skiing
  • Powerful forward-lean mechanisms lock you in between 15deg - 22deg; release the forward lean for a comfortable walk mode while hiking or strolling around the lodge
  • Scarpa T1 telemark boots sport rockerless Vibram Escape soles that provide excellent responsiveness while turning
