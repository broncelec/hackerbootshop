Built to keep your feet warm and comfortable, the women's Nordica Cruise 65 ski boots are a great choice for fun on the mountain this season

  • As compared to men's liners, women-specific liners feature extra padding in the tongues, more insulation throughout and contoured top edges designed to fit women's calves
  • Dual-density plastic maximizes ease of entry regardless of temperature; even if you have high insteps, getting your foot in the boot is hassle free
  • Soft shell flaps make it easy to get your foot in the boot; no more standing on 1 foot, hunching over, forcing your instep between 2 unforgiving, pinching plastic flaps
  • Liners snugly wrap the feet in cushioning foam and warm fleece lining; closed-cell foam against the shell enhances warmth
  • Liners provide a personalized fit around heel, ankle and center of foot when custom molded
  • Footbeds optimize foot position to create an effortless skiing stance, and they offer easy support for comfortable wear all day long

  • Power straps adjust cuffs for snugness and pressure against the shins
