Comfortable during the hike and powerful on the descent, the Salomon Quest 120 ski boots help you access the best part of the resort

  • When you're ready to ski, the cuffs lock directly into the lower shells to maximize power transmission on the descent
  • Innovative V-shaped lower shells and magnesium backbones virtually eliminate flex when backbones are engaged

  • Liners may be custom molded to enhance fit

  • Single canting sets cuffs to a different forward lean angles depending on how aggressively you ski
