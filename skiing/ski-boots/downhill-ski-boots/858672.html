Description:

Responsive and smooth, the advanced-level Dalbello KR Two Core ski boots offer the precision to aggressively romp down the mountain with ease thanks to snug lasts and anatomic boot molds

  • 98mm footbeds provide a responsive, precision fit designed for discriminating feet, but thanks to a new boot mold, the boots don't squeeze your feet like a vice
  • Boot mold enhances power, precision and comfort by matching 4 critical fit points: the heels, ankles, 5th metatarsals near your toes and the naviculars near insteps
  • Offering a precise foothold and a progressive forward flex, 3-piece shell optimizes responsiveness for today's relatively short, wide skis
  • Separate cuff, shell and tongue sections are joined together to create a smooth forward flex with precise side-to-side support
  • Enhancing side-to-side response and providing a snugger wrap, bi-injected cuffs feature stiff plastic on sides and soft plastic on the straps that wrap around shins
  • Medium-flex tongues enhance responsiveness, control and sensitivity to skis and snow surface
  • Liners feature soft comfort foam around the rearfoot areas and tongues, and the toe boxes stretch to enhance comfort and fit
  • Liners are engineered to fit correctly right out of the box, but they can be custom formed as well, a procedure best performed in an REI specialty ski shop
  • Twin canting adjusts the cuffs to fit the angles of your lower legs, levels the soles of the boots above the skis and offers a versatile range of adjustment
  • Flex index of 110 offers the responsive power transfer preferred by advanced skiers
