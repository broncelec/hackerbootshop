Description:

The Rossignol BC X6 75mm backcountry touring boots provide durability and warmth for exploring off the beaten path with warm Thinsulate(R) linings, durable lace covers and gaiters and rugged soles

  • Quick-cinch external cuff gaiters and ultra-durable Cordura(R) nylon lace covers ensure the spindrift stays out so your feet stay dry and warm
  • Thinsulate(TM) polyester/thermo-moldable foam insulation supplies non-bulky warmth and comfort even when damp; after a few days of skiing the foam molds to your feet
  • Nylon shanks in the midsoles provide increased support, stability and durability, and help prevent foot fatigue; dual-density heel counters support and stabilize heels
  • Medial and lateral webbing reinforcements integrated into the boots' lacing system securely wrap the feet and lock down heels and insteps, maximizing support and control
  • Rossignol BC X6 ski boots have rugged, non-slip 75mm soles that fit 3-pin backcountry bindings
