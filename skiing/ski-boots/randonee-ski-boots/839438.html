Description:

The lightweight women's Dynafit One U-TF randonee boots balance a progressive flex with great walkability so you can ski those steep backcountry lines you've always dreamed about

  • Polyurethane shells and cuffs offer similar performance to the popular TLT5 Mountain boots, but are a touch wider in the forefoot for a more comfortable fit
  • Women-specific cuff design is lower and wider than the men's version to better accommodate the shape of women's lower legs
  • Expanded polyurethane insulation layer is glued to the insides of the shells to help keep your feet warm on frigid winter days
  • Compact shells with rockered soles provide excellent walkability and nimbleness when you're booting up steep snow slopes or scrambling rocky ridges
  • Lightweight women-specific liners are premolded on anatomic lasts for use right out of the box; or, have the liners custom molded to your feet by a qualified ski shop employee
  • Opening the Ultra-Lock magnesium cuff buckles simultaneously changes the boots from ski to walk mode for fast, effortless transitions
  • Central magnesium buckles are positioned exactly on the shell pivot points to keep your heels in place when you're skinning uphill and skiing back down
  • Cuff and central buckles are designed with a retention system to prevent unwanted release
  • Ski/walk mechanisms can be set between 15deg and 18deg of forward lean, as well as loosened for walking
  • Flexible tongue design doesn't interfere with the cuff rotation for comfort when touring
  • Power straps help close the boots securely around your legs
  • Dynafit One U-TF randonee boots have self-guiding notches located at the inserts at the toes of the boots to ease stepping into Dynafit bindings
