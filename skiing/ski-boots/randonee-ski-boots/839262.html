Description:

Fly down backcountry runs and tour back up for more with the women's Scarpa Skadi randonee boots

  • They are designed for excellent performance on both the dowhill and uphill sections of your tour
  • Polymer shells and Pebax(R) plastic tongues provide a good balance of stiffness and flexibility for comfort while backcountry skiing
  • Dual-density V-frame ribs on the cuffs enhance the power of the boots
  • Skadi boots have a forefoot width of 101mm; women-specific fit includes narrower heels, lower cuffs at the calves and higher insteps than comparable men's boots
  • Touring-style liners offer good range of motion yet provide support with stiff tongues
  • Tongue-style liners (as opposed to a wrap-style liners) provide easy entry and comfortable flex through the shins when touring
  • Cocona(R) lining helps move moisture and reduce odors; Cocona is made with fibers from activated carbon from coconut shells
  • See an REI ski shop professional to have the liners custom molded to your feet to maximize comfort and performance
  • Boots are compatible with all randonee bindings; tech fittings at the toes accept Dynafit-style bindings
  • Tech fittings have grooves that make getting into Dynafit-style bindings easy; fitting indicators can be seen from above to help you line the boot toes up with the bindings
  • 3 Wiregate buckles and an instep strap on each boot close securely around your feet and legs; clasps hold buckles in place for quick transitions between uphill and downhill
  • Wide rip-and-stick power straps wrap securely around the outside of the cuffs, distributing pressure and bringing the cuffs as close as possible your lower legs
  • Rear ski/walk mechanisms free the cuffs for walking comfort; flip the switches to ski mode and you can lock the cuffs at 16deg or 20deg of forward lean
  • Scarpa Skadi randonee boots feature lugged Vibram soles for good grip while hiking on rock and snow
