Description:

As classic touring skis, the Black Diamond Stigma telemark/randonee skis are lightweight, nimble-but-stable performers from the skin track to tight, technical couloirs

  • Part of the Tour series, the Stigma specializes in long approaches and technical descents on both soft and hard snow
  • Lightweight paulownia wood core is surrounded by Torsion Box construction and carbon fiber reinforcements
  • Torsion Box technology wraps fiberglass around the core like a burrito, creating excellent torsional power and energy pop
  • Dual Torsion Bow(R) construction features two ribs that run the length of each ski to distribute loads
  • Soft tip flex pattern and a tight turning radius with 80mm waists make the Stigma skis ideal for quick turns on variable snow
  • Feature flat tails you can easily plunge into firm snow when building anchors or staking out a tent
  • Stainless-steel notches at the tails keep climbing skins (not included) securely attached and centered
  • Black Diamond Stigma skis have high-speed sintered bases that feature a cross-hatch stone grind for a balance of durability, glide and maintenance
