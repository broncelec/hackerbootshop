Description:

Suited to intermediate skiers pushing their skills on groomed runs, the Volkl RTM 75 iS skis with bindings inspire speedy confidence thanks to a lively core and easy-to-turn rockered tips

  • You'll notice a snappy feel when linking turns thanks to the dual wood core-it's what makes the Volkl RTM 75 iS skis a touch more nimble than the RTM 75 skis
  • Very stable and responsive at speed, the dual wood core offers a forgiving feel that won't punish you for small mistakes in technique
  • Raised reinforcement channels along the top edges of the skis, a distinct feature on all Volkl carvers, also make the skis feel more stable at higher speeds
  • Slightly rockered tips make it easy to start turning at a moment's notice and offer forgiving stability in soft snow and crud
  • Soft flexing tail makes it easy to release skis out of turns and avoid oncoming obstacles, a welcome feature if you are tackling tough slopes
  • Bindings attach to skis via integrated rails, eliminating screws and promoting superb edge hold and natural flex
  • Integrated bindings features DIN 3 - 10 release settings, a range suited to beginning and intermediate skiers at low to medium speeds
  • Base or topsheet color may vary from online photo
