Description:

With 108mm waists to shred off-piste snow with stability and a rockered profile to get the most out of deep snow, the Salomon Rocker 2 108 skis are powder planks you can ski any day

  • 108mm waists offer more than enough flotation for epic days in bottomless powder, and they are stable enough to easily maneuver over rough, crusty, and windblown snow
  • Powder-rockered tips and tails deliver maximum flotation and excellent performance at high speeds
  • When put on edge, about 80% of the skis' edge length contacts the snow to make groomer skiing easy
  • Designed for demanding skiers who require high performance, full wood core delivers direct transmission of power with precision and efficiency
  • Perfect for pulling off freeski maneuvers, a single-layer cap design provides precise edge grip and efficient energy transfer from skier to snow
