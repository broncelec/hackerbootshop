Description:

Fast and smooth at speed, the advanced-level Atomic Smoke Ti ARC skis with bindings blaze down groomed runs with unwavering stability thanks to 76mm waists and a metal laminate in the core

  • Titanium laminate enhances stability and boosts performance at high speeds
  • 76mm waists offer superior maneuverability on groomed runs
  • When skis are unweighted, there is a slight rise in the tips that makes it easy to initiate turns
  • When skis are put on edge after starting a turn, the camber flattens out to deliver full-length contact with snow and excellent edge hold
  • Microcellular composite core is lighter and more lively than wood, and it reduces swing weight for effortless handling
  • Half-cap construction in tips and tails improves durability, and sidewalls in binding area enhance edge grip by absorbing shock
  • Sidewall construction ensures maximum torsional rigidity, uncanny edge grip and controlled tip-to-tail flex; sidewall is reinforced in binding area with 2 layers of material
  • Integrated bindings allow the skis to flex naturally for excellent edge contact and bump absorption
  • Bindings feature DIN 4 - 12 release settings, a range suited to advanced skiers at medium to high speeds
