Description:

Marker Jester Schizo B110 downhill ski bindings improve freestyle performance by offering an adjustable interface to cater to the type of skiing you choose

  • Whole binding moves 3cm forward or 3cm back using a simple screw adjustment on the toe piece
  • Are you spending time in the park or riding switch? Position the binding in the forward position to maximize the skis' responsiveness
  • Or is the rest of the mountain calling your name with its fresh snowfall? Position the binding in the rear position and enjoy a back-seat ride
  • Featuring updated magnesium parts to increase durability and control in 2012/2013, the Jester Schizo bindings enhance control of wide-waisted skis
  • Burly DIN settings from 6-16 lets hard-charging skiers stomp huge landings and hit mach speeds
  • Cross-axis toe spring and compact mounting create a short binding that enables fluid spins and twists
  • Triple Pivot Elite toe absorbs high levels of energy to help prevent prerelease
  • Lightweight Inter-Pivot heel focuses power in the direction of the boot sole and offers superior holding power and energy transfer
  • Freeride brakes ensure adequate clearance when skiing backward
  • The Marker Jester Schizo B110 downhill ski bindings feature a heel piece without pull-out screws to maximize durability
  • Contact points located directly under the sole holder efficiently transfer power to the ski
  • Stainless-steel anti-friction device allows essential lateral boot movement despite the condition of dirty or worn soles or negative sliding friction
  • 110mm brake fits all-mountain wide skis and some powder skis
