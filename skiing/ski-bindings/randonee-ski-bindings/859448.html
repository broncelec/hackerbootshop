Description:

With the feel of alpine bindings and the ability to tour the backcountry, the burly Look XM 16 S C130 randonee bindings let you charge down the steepest lines and climb back up for more

  • Low binding height, a wide footprint and very high stiffness create the same performance as an alpine binding to give you the confidence to ski the most challenging terrain
  • Switching from touring mode to ski mode and back again is accomplished with a simple flick of your ski pole and can be done with the skis on your feet
  • Hard-charging skiers who like to hit jumps and cruise at high speeds will appreciate the adjustable DIN that ranges from 7 to 16
  • Bindings have a low overall stand height for great stability while skiing
  • Bindings include 130mm wide brakes to fit your fat powder skis
  • Flip the climbing bars up with a ski pole to take some stress off your legs when you're skinning uphill; two different positions let you choose the height that works for you
  • Look XM 16 S C130 randonee bindings fit boot sizes with sole lengths ranging from 265 - 320mm; bindings are only compatible with boots with alpine DIN soles or WTR soles
