Go from the resort straight to the backcountry with the Salomon Guardian WTR 13L randonee bindings

  • They have the feel of alpine bindings, with the benefit of being able to tour in the backcountry
  • Switching from hike to ski mode shouldn't be a hassle, and it isn't with the Guardian bindings-simply flick the switches with your ski poles to change modes
  • Aggressive skiers who like to cruise at high speeds will appreciate the adjustable DIN that ranges from 5 to 13
  • Bindings have a low overall stand height of 26mm for great stability while skiing
  • 80mm width transmits power from your boots to your wide skis for great downhill performance
