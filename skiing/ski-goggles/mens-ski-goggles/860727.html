Description:

The Smith I/O Violet Ombre snow goggles give you a wide, distortion-free view and let you adjust for different conditions by swapping between the interchangeable Blackout and Blue Sensor lenses

  • Blackout lens features a dark gray tint and 10% visible light transission (VLT) to cut glare and reduce eye fatigue in bright, sunny conditions
  • Blue Sensor Mirror lens features a light rose tint and 70% VLT to maximize color definition and increase depth perception in flat light
  • To quickly adjust for different lighting or terrain, simply flip the two clips at the top of the frame, pop out the first lens and replace it with the new one
  • Spherical lens tapers from the optical center toward the periphery for a wide field of vision with minimal distortion
  • Double-layer thermal lens features a microporous port that allows lens to breathe while preventing moisture entry, greatly reducing fogging
  • Lenses also feature a Smith 5X Anti-Fog treatment to withstand fogging longer than standard anti-fog lenses
  • 3-layer face foam is constructed of a dense base, a plush mid layer and a soft fleece top layer; design ensures a tight seal, good moisture management and maximum comfort
  • Extra-wide adjustable strap is backed with a silicone bead so it stays in place on your helmet; clip buckle makes for easy on and off over a helmet or hat
  • The Smith I/O snow goggles are designed for medium-size faces and come with a microfiber storage bag with replacement lens sleeve
