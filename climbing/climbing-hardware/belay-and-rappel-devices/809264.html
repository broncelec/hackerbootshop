Description:

The Petzl Universo belay system includes a Verso belay device and an Attache 3D carabiner with sliding connection piece that keeps the belay device and carabiner properly oriented

  • Connection piece on the Attache 3D carabiner allows the Verso belay device to remain in position and the carabiner to stay properly oriented along its major axis
  • Connection piece also limits the chance of dropping your belay device during rope changes
  • Verso belay device features V-shaped channels that increase friction on thin ropes; works with twin ropes, half ropes and single ropes
  • Verso is compatible with twin ropes 7

  • 9mm in diameter and larger

  • Manual screwgate system features a red band indicator to show when the carabiner is unlocked, providing a quick visual safety check
