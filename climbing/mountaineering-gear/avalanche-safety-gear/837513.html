Description:

The Black Diamond Outlaw Avalung ski pack is ideal for backcountry snowplay

  • It can increase the time you have to breathe if trapped in an avalanche
  • Integrated AvaLung apparatus lets you breathe air directly from the snowpack via a mouthpiece
  • Exhaled carbon dioxide is directed out the exhaust port at the bottom right of the pack, extending the time available before an ice layer builds up and seals out oxygen
  • Breathing tube runs through the left shoulder strap and can be positioned such that the mouthpiece is readily available
  • Suspension offers full range of motion, and optimal comfort for scrambling and climbing
  • Shoulder straps move in concert with your stride and reach by sliding through the bottom of the pack with a low-friction cable and housing
  • Shoulder straps are linked to together by the cable which helps maintain an even, balanced load across the shoulders
  • Front and back panel access to main compartment lets you get to your gear quickly and easily, even with skis or a snowboard attached
  • Dedicated avalanche tool pocket keeps a shovel and probe accessible
  • Insulated sleeve helps prevent your hydration reservoir (sold separately) from freezing up; drinking tube routes through insulated right shoulder strap
  • Hipbelt stash pocket keeps energy bars and other small items within reach
  • Bag is designed to carry skis A-frame or diagonal; also accommodates a snowboard
  • Note: The Black Diamond Outlaw AvaLung pack is not a substitute for wise assessment of hazardous avalanche terrain
